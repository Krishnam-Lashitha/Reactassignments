Amsterdam Light Festival

The Amsterdam Light Festival is a 53-day event that takes place in the city center of Amsterdam. It features light art installations created by international artists, designers, and architects. The festival is free and open to the public.

When does the festival take place?

  • The 13th edition of the festival, Rituals, took place from November 28, 2024 to January 19, 2025.
  • The next edition will take place from November 27, 2025 to January 18, 2026.

How can I experience the festival?

  • Walk, bike, or take a boat to see the light art installations.
  • Book a canal cruise, open boat tour, or private tour.
  • Join a group event.

What can I expect to see?

  • Light art installations that illuminate the city center.
  • Light artworks that can be seen on the canals, by bike, or on foot.
  • Light artworks by artists from around the world.
  • Light artworks that are tailor-made for the festival.
